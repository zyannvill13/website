Characters of Jujutsu Kaisen

Yuji Itadori

Yuji is the main protagonist of Jujutsu Kaisen. He is a high school student who becomes a jujutsu sorcerer.

Megumi Fushiguro

Megumi is a jujutsu sorcerer and a classmate of Yuji. He uses shikigami to fight curses.

Nobara Kugisaki

Nobara is a confident jujutsu sorcerer who wields a hammer and nails to combat curses.

Ryomen Sukuna

Sukuna is the King of Curses, feared by all. He resides within Yuji Itadori's body.

Satoru Gojo

Gojo is a powerful jujutsu sorcerer and a teacher at the Tokyo Metropolitan Curse Technical College.
